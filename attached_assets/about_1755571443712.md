# Medicaid Audit Intelligence — About

## Purpose
Medicaid Audit Intelligence centralizes recently published Medicaid-related audit reports for program managers, auditors, and others who want a wide-angle view of issues reported by oversight agencies.

## What this tool does
The platform turns unstructured audit PDFs into structured records you can search, compare, and use to power downstream processes (dashboards, a reference library, and more).

## How AI is used
All data elements are extracted by OpenAI models from publicly available audit reports. Most fields—such as **objective**, **overall conclusion**, and **findings**—are captured as close to the original wording as possible. Two fields are intentionally AI-generated summaries: **AI Scope Summary** and **AI Overall Conclusion**.

## Data quality & verification
AI systems can make mistakes. Links to the original source documents are provided for verification, and corrections are welcome. If you spot an error in the extracted data, please email **[EMAIL HERE]**.

## Extraction workflow (high level)
1. **Report collection:** Relevant Medicaid audit reports are compiled from state and federal oversight sites.  
2. **Model extraction:** Each report is processed by two lightweight OpenAI models to extract fields according to a predefined schema.  
3. **Normalization:** Outputs are standardized (e.g., dates, agency names) for consistent search and comparison.  
4. **Review & publish:** Records are checked and published with citations back to the source PDF.
